# Content Attribution and Source Transparency Policy

## Page 1

CONTENT ATTRIBUTION AND SOURCE
TRANSPARENCY POLICY
Effective Date: June 15, 2026
1. Purpose
NewsIQ is an AI-powered news intelligence platform designed to help users understand stories and events.
NewsIQ is not a publisher of original reporting and does not seek to replace journalism.
Original reporting remains essential to an informed society, and NewsIQ seeks to complement rather than
substitute the work of publishers.
2. Attribution Principles
NewsIQ is guided by principles of:
Source transparency;
Publisher recognition;
Fair attribution;
User understanding;
Respect for intellectual property.
3. Ownership of Publisher Content
Copyright and ownership of articles, images, trademarks, and other materials belong to their respective
publishers and rights holders.
NewsIQ does not claim ownership of third-party articles.
Nothing in the Services transfers ownership rights to NewsIQ or its users.

4. Source Identification
Where possible, NewsIQ endeavors to display:
Publisher names;
Article titles;
Publication information;
Links to original sources.
Users are encouraged to visit and support original publishers.
5. AI Summaries
NewsIQ may generate:
Headlines;
One-line summaries;
Detailed summaries;
Timelines;
Fact extractions;
Source comparisons;
Difference analyses.
These outputs are intended to help users understand stories and identify important developments.
AI-generated outputs are not intended to replace original reporting.
6. Source Diversity
NewsIQ seeks to expose users to information originating from multiple publishers and perspectives.
Source diversity may improve understanding and reduce overreliance on any single publication.
7. Fair Use and Fair Dealing
NewsIQ seeks to operate in accordance with applicable copyright laws and recognized principles of:
Fair use;
Fair dealing;
Attribution;

8. AI Limitations
AI systems may:
Misinterpret source materials;
Omit relevant facts;
Produce incomplete summaries;
Reflect ambiguity in reporting.
Users should consult original sources when accuracy, completeness, or timeliness is important.
9. Publisher Concerns
Publishers and rights holders with concerns relating to:
Attribution;
Source display;
Copyright matters;
Content usage;
may contact:
support@newsiq.ai
NewsIQ seeks to address concerns in good faith.
10. Transparency Commitments
NewsIQ aims to:
Clearly distinguish AI-generated content from publisher content;
Encourage source verification;
Promote understanding rather than replacement of journalism;
Respect publisher rights;
Improve transparency over time.

11. Commitment to Journalism
NewsIQ recognizes that original journalism is fundamental to democratic societies and informed public
discourse.
The purpose of NewsIQ is to enhance comprehension and context, not to diminish the importance of
publishers or original reporting.
NewsIQ seeks to foster an ecosystem in which users, publishers, and AI technologies can coexist in a
manner that promotes transparency, attribution, and informed understanding.
