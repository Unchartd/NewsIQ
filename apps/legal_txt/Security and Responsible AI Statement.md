SECURITY AND RESPONSIBLE AI STATEMENT
Effective Date: June 15, 2026
1. Introduction
NewsIQ is committed to building trustworthy, secure, and responsible AI-powered tools that help users
better understand the news.
Our goal is to improve comprehension, context, and transparency while respecting user privacy, publisher
rights, and the limitations of artificial intelligence systems.
2. Security Principles
NewsIQ follows security principles designed to protect users, systems, and information.
Our security objectives include:
Confidentiality;
Integrity;
Availability;
Resilience;
Data minimization.
Security is incorporated throughout the design, development, and operation of the Services.
3. Technical Safeguards
NewsIQ may employ measures including:
Encryption
HTTPS encryption;
TLS transport security;
Encryption in transit;
Encryption of sensitive information where appropriate.
Authentication
Secure login mechanisms;
Session protection;

6. Human Oversight
Artificial intelligence systems are designed to assist users rather than replace human judgment.
Users remain responsible for evaluating information and making decisions based on their own judgment.
NewsIQ encourages users to consult original sources and verify important information independently.
7. AI Limitations
AI systems are probabilistic and inherently imperfect.
Outputs may:
Contain inaccuracies;
Omit relevant facts;
Misinterpret context;
Produce outdated information;
Reflect ambiguity in source materials.
AI-generated outputs should not be considered definitive or authoritative.
8. Bias and Fairness
NewsIQ recognizes that AI systems may exhibit limitations or unintended biases.
NewsIQ continuously seeks to improve:
Neutrality;
Transparency;
Contextual understanding;
Source diversity.
Complete elimination of bias is not possible, and users should consider multiple sources when evaluating
information.

9. Transparency
NewsIQ strives to provide transparency regarding:
AI-generated summaries;
Source attribution;
Comparison tools;
Timeline generation;
Difference analysis.
Where feasible, users are encouraged to access original publisher content.
10. Security Incident Response
NewsIQ maintains procedures intended to:
Detect threats;
Investigate incidents;
Mitigate risks;
Restore operations;
Comply with applicable notification obligations.
11. Continuous Improvement
Security and AI systems evolve over time.
NewsIQ continuously reviews:
Infrastructure;
AI systems;
Privacy practices;
Safety measures;
Monitoring capabilities.
12. No Absolute Guarantees
No system can guarantee complete security or perfect accuracy.
