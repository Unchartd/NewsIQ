COPYRIGHT AND DMCA POLICY
Effective Date: June 15, 2026
NewsIQ respects intellectual property rights and expects users and third parties to do the same.
NewsIQ is an AI-powered news intelligence platform that aggregates and analyzes information from third-
party publishers. NewsIQ does not claim ownership of original news articles or reporting.
1. Copyright Ownership
Ownership of articles, images, trademarks, logos, and other materials remains with their respective owners
and publishers.
NewsIQ seeks to:
Attribute publishers;
Provide links to original articles;
Respect publisher rights;
Operate in accordance with applicable copyright laws and fair use principles.
2. Attribution Practices
NewsIQ endeavors to:
Identify original publishers;
Display source names;
Provide direct links to source websites;
Encourage users to visit publisher websites.
NewsIQ does not represent third-party content as its own.
3. Reporting Copyright Claims
Copyright owners who believe material available through NewsIQ infringes their rights may submit a notice
to:
support@newsiq.ai
