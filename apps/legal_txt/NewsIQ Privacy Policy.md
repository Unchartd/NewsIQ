# NewsIQ Privacy Policy

## Page 1

4. Personalization and Profiling
NewsIQ is designed to help users understand important stories and discover information that is most
relevant to them.
To provide a personalized experience, NewsIQ may use:
Reading history.
Topics viewed.
Search activity.
Categories followed.
Bookmarks.
Engagement metrics.
Story interactions.
Time spent reading content.
These signals may be used to:
Generate personalized feeds.
Rank stories.
Recommend topics.
Prioritize content.
Improve search results.
Surface related articles.
This processing may constitute profiling under certain privacy laws.
NewsIQ does not use profiling to make decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on
individuals.
Where required by applicable law, users may object to or opt out of certain personalization activities.
5. AI Processing
NewsIQ uses artificial intelligence technologies to help users understand and analyze news stories.
AI capabilities may include:
Headline generation.
One-line summaries.
Detailed summaries.
Timeline generation.
Fact extraction.

5.1 Third-Party AI Providers
NewsIQ may use third-party large language models and AI services, including:
OpenAI.

Other AI providers as our infrastructure evolves.
5.2 Information Processed by AI Systems
To generate outputs, NewsIQ may provide AI systems with:
Article text.
Headlines.
Publication metadata.
Source information.
Story context.
NewsIQ seeks to minimize personal information sent to AI providers.
Where possible, NewsIQ aims to avoid transmitting user-specific information that is not necessary for
providing AI functionality.
5.3 AI Limitations
Artificial intelligence systems are probabilistic and may:
Produce inaccurate information.
Omit important details.
Generate incomplete summaries.
Reflect outdated information.
Misinterpret context.
Users should independently verify important facts and decisions.

6. Data Minimization
NewsIQ seeks to collect and process only information reasonably necessary for:
Operating the Services.
Improving user experiences.
Personalization.
Security.
Legal compliance.
We strive to avoid collecting excessive information and regularly evaluate our data practices.
7. Redis Sessions and Authentication
NewsIQ uses session technologies, including Redis, to:
Manage authentication.
Maintain active sessions.
Improve performance.
Protect account security.
Session identifiers are used to authenticate users and support secure access to the Services.
Redis session data is generally temporary and retained only as necessary for operational purposes.
8. Cookies and Similar Technologies
NewsIQ may use cookies and similar technologies to support:
Essential Functions
Login sessions.
Security.
Authentication.
Functional Features
Theme preferences.
Language settings.
User preferences.

12. Information Sharing
