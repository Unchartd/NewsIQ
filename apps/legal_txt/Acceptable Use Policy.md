ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY
Effective Date: June 15, 2026
This Acceptable Use Policy governs the use of NewsIQ and supplements the Terms of Service.
1. Lawful Use
Users must use NewsIQ only in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Users may not:

Including:
Fraud;
Identity theft;
Money laundering;
Harassment;
Unlawful activities.
2.2 Abuse Accounts
Including:
Credential sharing;
Unauthorized account transfers;
Subscription abuse;
Circumventing usage limits.
2.3 Security Violations
Users may not:
Probe vulnerabilities;
