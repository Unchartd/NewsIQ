1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites and applications.
Cookies help websites:
Remember user preferences;
Maintain login sessions;
Improve performance;
Provide security;
Analyze usage patterns.
Cookies may be temporary ("session cookies") or persistent ("persistent cookies").
2. Technologies We Use
NewsIQ may use:
Browser cookies;
Session tokens;
Local storage;
Device identifiers;
Similar technologies used for authentication and analytics.
