# Data Retention and Privacy Policy

## Page 1

14. Data Retention
NewsIQ retains information only for as long as reasonably necessary to:
Provide the Services;
Maintain account functionality;
Improve products and features;
Detect fraud and abuse;
Comply with legal obligations;
Resolve disputes;
Enforce agreements.
Retention periods vary depending on the type of information involved.
14.1 Account Information
Account information may be retained while your account remains active and for a reasonable period
thereafter , as required for operational, legal, and security purposes.
14.2 Behavioral Data
Reading history, searches, bookmarks, and engagement information may be retained to:
Personalize feeds;
Improve recommendations;
Analyze product usage;
Enhance user experiences.
Certain behavioral information may be aggregated or anonymized.
14.3 Logs and Security Records
Logs, audit records, and security-related information may be retained for fraud prevention,
troubleshooting, abuse detection, and legal compliance.
14.4 Backups
Backups may persist for a limited period after deletion requests due to technical and disaster recovery
requirements.

15. Account Deletion
Users may request deletion of their account.
Subject to legal obligations and legitimate business purposes, NewsIQ will delete or anonymize personal
information within a reasonable period.
Deletion requests may not immediately remove:
Archived backups;
Security records;
Information required by law;
Fraud prevention records;
Information necessary to resolve disputes.
16. Data Portability
Where required by applicable law, users may request a copy of certain personal information in a structured,
commonly used, and machine-readable format.
17. Your Rights Under GDPR and UK GDPR
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:
Right of Access
You may request access to personal data held by NewsIQ.
Right to Rectification
You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
Right to Erasure
You may request deletion of personal information under certain circumstances.

27. Additional Information for AI Processing
NewsIQ uses artificial intelligence systems to enhance understanding of news and events.
AI-generated outputs are probabilistic and may:
Contain inaccuracies;
Omit relevant facts;
Become outdated;
Misinterpret context;
Produce incomplete summaries.
NewsIQ does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of AI-generated outputs.
Users should independently verify important information.
AI-generated outputs are intended for informational purposes only and should not be relied upon as:
Legal advice;
Financial advice;
Investment advice;
Medical advice;
Professional advice.
NewsIQ is an AI-powered news intelligence platform and not a publisher of original journalism.
Ownership and copyright in third-party news articles remain with their respective publishers and rights
holders.
