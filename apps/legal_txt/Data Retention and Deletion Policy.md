# Data Retention and Deletion Policy

## Page 1

DATA RETENTION AND DELETION POLICY
Effective Date: June 15, 2026
1. Purpose
This Policy explains how NewsIQ retains and deletes information.
NewsIQ aims to retain information only for as long as necessary to:
Provide Services;
Improve products;
Maintain security;
Comply with legal obligations.
2. Account Data
Account information may include:
Email addresses;
Profile information;
Preferences;
Subscription details.
Such information is generally retained while accounts remain active.
3. Behavioral Data
NewsIQ may retain:
Reading history;
Search history;
Topics viewed;
Bookmarks;
Engagement information.
Behavioral information supports:
Personalization;

4. Logs and Security Records
Logs may be retained for:
Monitoring;
Abuse detection;
Fraud prevention;
Troubleshooting;
Legal compliance.
5. Backup Systems
Information stored in backup systems may persist temporarily after deletion requests due to disaster
recovery requirements.
6. Account Deletion Requests
Users may request deletion of their account.
Subject to legal obligations, NewsIQ will delete or anonymize personal information within a reasonable
period.
Certain records may be retained where required for:
Fraud prevention;
Security;
Legal compliance;
Dispute resolution.
7. Data Portability
Where required by law, users may request a copy of eligible information in a structured and machine-
readable format.

NewsIQ may update this Policy periodically.
Continued use of the Services constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
